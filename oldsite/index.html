A return to Intentional Living

The world is a degenerating and confused mess, and the commonplace “solutions” are not working. We don’t have to settle for living in a mess, with solutions that don’t work. We don’t have to settle for unhealthy food, poor government education, complicated economic structures, and division and social isolation. There are people that want to create something better and different. We can grow our own healthy food, so we feel better, and aren’t dependent on the conventional food supply chain. We can build a different educational environment for our children at home or private schools. We can build a counter-economy for trade and business transactions. We can create a true community of caring and like minded people.

We are people that want the same things you do. Come join us.
